Welcome

Welcome to our beta book sharing website.

Over here you can sell all of your old textbooks, as well as buy new ones you might need.

When you click on the books catalog you will see a huge array of used books. To see in more detail each book click on the name tag to go inot the details page.

If you want to buy a book, simply look through the books catalog for a book you want.

If find such a satisfactory book contact the seller through the various contact methods they have put out.

If you want to sell a book click on the big white plus sign on the top right of the screen.

Put in all the information that is required. You can edit your book details at anytime.

If someone buys your book, make sure to delete the book from the catalog by going into the books detail page.
